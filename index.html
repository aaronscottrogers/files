Governance & Policy Tailwinds
External policy drivers creating sustained demand across target markets
🇬🇧
UK Agent Quality Framework (AQF)
All UK education providers on Student sponsor register must manage agents in compliance with National Code of Ethical Practice. IELTS alignment positions British Council as governance leader vs competitors lacking formal compliance frameworks.
📋
UK Skilled Worker Visa — B2 Uplift
English requirement raised to B2 level (CEFR) effective January 2026 for new applicants, up from B1. Translates to IELTS score of 5.5 minimum in all four components, creating a clear and growing preparation market.
🍁
Canada Express Entry
IELTS General Training accepted for permanent residence with results valid for 2 years. IELTS remains dominant over CELPIP and TEF Canada alternatives, driving sustained demand particularly in South Asian markets.
🦘
Australia Skilled Migration
Competent English requirements for skilled migration recognise IELTS as the primary assessment tool, driving sustained demand across all 8 target markets, particularly Bangladesh, Pakistan, and Vietnam.
